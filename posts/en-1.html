Login tutorial
Notice
In this post, I will let you know steps to login into MonokaiToolkit.

First thing you have to make sure is the account you're going to login has been logged into this device at least once. If you don't try to login Facebook using your default browser. Otherwise, Facebook App is also acceptable.

Another thing to care about is if you don't use this device regularly, please be careful since it may cause Checkpoint.
If you want a safe login method, please use MonokaiToolkit 2 Extension (Chrome Extension) and use Login with QR feature.
Login instruction
Step 1: Click Add/remove account (Or Login button, any feature) on the top of Home screen.
Step 2: Click Add account then click Login with Facebook/Login with QR.
Step 3: If you use Normal Login feature, please login with Facebook into new popup that has just appeared.
Step 3.1: Surf Facebook for a couple minutes (Avoid Checkpoint), then click Cross button (Android) on the top-right of screen or Done (iOS) on the bottom of screen.
Step 3.2: Application will automatically make the progress done and you're now ready to use the app.
Step 4: If you use Login with QR method, please make sure you're bringing your laptop/PC with you and it must have your Facebook account logged in!
Step 4.1: Open chrome, open Google Search then search for: "MonokaiToolkit 2 Extension" (Remember that you have to login your Facebook on Chrome first).
Step 4.2: Click MonokaiToolkit icon on Extension bar, on the new page, click QR Code at User Card.
Step 4.3: Return to the app, click Login with QR and allow the app to use your camera. Finally, scan the QR and wait for a few seconds. It will be done soon.
